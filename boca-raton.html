Boca Raton

location > Boca Raton, Florida

interior design > MILA DESIGN

project type > Residential

-

This kitchen is a masterclass in Modern Minimalism, where organic textures meet a cool, architectural palette. The design creates a serene, high-end atmosphere through its clean lines and clever use of contrasting materials.

-

Design Highlights

Materials & Textures: The space features a sophisticated interplay between the warmth of vertical-grain oak cabinetry and the sleek, sage green matte finishes of the perimeter units.

The Island: A stunning focal point, the island features a sculptural curved timber base that softens the room's geometric precision.

Seamless Integration: The appliances are hidden behind custom millwork, while the induction cooktop sits flush with the countertop.

Architectural Accents: A matte black minimalist gooseneck faucet adds a sharp, graphic contrast against the light-toned island.

Neutral Foundation: Large-format concrete flooring provides a neutral, industrial-inspired base.

-

The Atmosphere

The overall mood is one of "Quiet Luxury." By removing visual noise and focusing on high-quality tactile finishes, the kitchen feels more like a curated gallery than a utility space.
